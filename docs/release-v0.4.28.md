- Adds Crosshair Studio with built-in and custom presets, folders, monitor controls, and one saved global toggle binding.

- Improves Driver Health and Latency readiness meters, guidance, and compact layouts.

- Sharpens typography, responsive sizing, navigation, Settings, and Support.
- Replaces V Rising with Palworld in the public leaderboard tracker.
- Makes Fair-Play submission handling more resilient while preserving audit evidence.
- Preserves updater identity, install paths, PresentMon repair, telemetry ABI, and user-triggered update behavior.
- Dev-signed release: SmartScreen / unknown-publisher prompts are still expected.
